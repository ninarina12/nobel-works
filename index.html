Nobel Constellations

Hover to reveal institutions with Nobel laureates in the surrounding discipline.

Click to reveal affiliated Nobel laureates and their total record of publications. Circle areas are scaled based on the laureate's total number of publications up through the year 2016.

Nobel Lights

Select an institution from the dropdown menu below to reveal the publication records of affiliated Nobel laureates over time. Publication data are aggregated for each laureate into 4-year bins from the year 1892 to 2016.

Nobel Stars

Prize-winning publication data for selected institutions. Each scattered point marks a prize-winning publication colored by the discipline in which the Nobel prize was awarded. The x-axis denotes the year of publication, and the y-axis indicates the year the Nobel prize was awarded. Click and drag an area selection to highlight the publications in the corresponding window across all institutions.

Data source: Li, J., Yin, Y., Fortunato, S. et al. A dataset of publication records for Nobel laureates. Sci Data 6, 33 (2019).
